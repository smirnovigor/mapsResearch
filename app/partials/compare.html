Conclusion ( There is two main options useful for our project )

Rich functionality:

In technical scope, Google maps is a winner. Great framework from Google that provide strong functionality and services, well documented, easy and intuitive API. The 80% of required functionality is already implemented in this example.


Free of charge:

For free use OpenLayer is the best options. For simple functionality a lightweight Leaflet is preferable option too. These JS frameworks in combination with MapQuest Tile and OpenStreetMap map service give worthy alternative to Google Maps.
